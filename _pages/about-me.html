To live is the rarest thing in the world, most people just exist

Oscar Wilde

Soumya Veer lives her life to the fullest. Her philosophy is to live in the moment and grab the day – Carpe diem!.

She is a lover of technology and is pursuing her career in Engineering. She has done her Masters in Electrical Engineering, has a work experience of three years in Infosys Ltd. and a bachelors in Electronics & Communications Engineering. Even though she was attracted towards Programming since she was a child, it took her a Master’s degree and a marriage to her Software Engineer husband to realize her passion for Software Development.

Her first love is singing. She is like a radio, you can just tune in and she will be on until told otherwise. Her interests also include writing, and miraculously, sometimes those drafts (the ones that satisfy her criteria of perfection) find a way from her desk to her blogs.

Her goal in life is to become an Encyclopedia. And she believes that it can be achieved by reading, reading and more reading. Her dream is to own the biggest library of books in the World.
